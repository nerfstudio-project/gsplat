Evaluation¶

We evaluate our implementation of Gaussian Splatting (Splatfacto) on the Mip-NeRF 360 dataset, benchmarking it against the original Inria method. We evaluate all methods with the same resolution (2x downscale) and COLMAP params/undistortion, and we report results at 7,000 and 30,000 steps. All evaluations were executed on an NVIDIA RTX 4090 GPU.
